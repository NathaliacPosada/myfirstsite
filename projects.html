About me

Hey, folks! I'm Nathalia Posada, a high-energy communicator with a blend of internship and hands-on experience, especially in the realms of communication, journalism, and design. I kicked off my journey in the hallways of a TV studio, delving into the intricacies of graphic design, marketing, and everything that makes the media world tick.

A graduate in Communication and Media from Polytechnic of Leiria, I'm currently diving headfirst into the first year of my Master's in Design and Multimedia at the University of Coimbra. My razor-sharp communication skills are getting a stylish upgrade with a dash of web design and graphic design.

And speaking of awesome projects, I had the pleasure of working and interning in the organization of the rhyme battle event in Leiria last year. It was an experience that not only tested my skills but also brought a lot of positive vibes to the local scene.

Outside the professional hustle, I'm a Brazilian lost in portuguese lands, living here for six years. I love to travel, savor new flavors, and, of course, I'm the proud mom of an adorable kitty.

I'm super pumped to continue this journey in the design universe, blending communication, multimedia, and loads of visual creativity. Come join me in exploring this world full of passions, projects, and positive vibes!
